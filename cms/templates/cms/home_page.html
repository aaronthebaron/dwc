About the Tech Workers Coalition

We are a progressive community of tech workers from the Bay Area. Our mission is to strengthen our local communities, to make our workplaces more inclusive, and to promote social responsibility.

By investing in the community, by building relationships with fellow workers outside the tech industry, and amplifying the voices for justice within our own industry, we aim to build a more positive, progressive San Francisco.
